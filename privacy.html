Privacy Policy

GreenArc Solutions LLC · Effective date: July 8, 2026

GreenArc Solutions LLC (“GreenArc Solutions,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website greenarcsolutions.com and provides web design and AI-powered business automation services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including when you:

  • Submit a form on our website (such as our contact or consultation request form) — this may include your name, email address, phone number, business name, and details about your inquiry.
  • Call, text, or email us directly.
  • Engage us as a client, in which case we collect the business information needed to deliver our services.

We also collect limited technical information automatically when you visit our website (such as browser type and pages visited) to understand site performance and improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the information or services you request.
  • To communicate with you about appointments, quotes, projects, and service updates.
  • To send text messages you have consented to receive (see “SMS/Text Messaging” below).
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

SMS/Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number through our website form and check the consent box, or verbally request text communication from us, you consent to receive text messages from GreenArc Solutions related to your inquiry, including responses to your questions, business information, quotes, and appointment scheduling.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

  • Message frequency varies based on your interaction with us.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at adam@greenarcsolutions.com.
  • Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate our business (such as website hosting, communications, and scheduling platforms), and only as necessary for them to perform those services on our behalf. These providers are not permitted to use your information for their own marketing. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law, and we use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information appropriately.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP.
  • Unsubscribe from emails using the link in any marketing email or by contacting us.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing us at adam@greenarcsolutions.com.

Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for businesses and individuals 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest revision. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
